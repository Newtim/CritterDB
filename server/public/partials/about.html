About

CritterDB is a web application built to help game masters find and create custom creatures and NPCs for the 5th edition of the world's greatest role playing game, right from your browser. After being frustrated by how time-consuming it was to create stat blocks for my own creatures I started searching around for a tool to help. Surprisingly, very few options existed, so I set out to create my own. The goal of CritterDB is to increase the quality of our creations, while also saving a ton of time by using CritterDB's easy guided creation system and by sharing our custom critters with each other.

On the home page you can browse through recent and featured bestiaries where you may find inspiration for your next fight or even an entire adventure.

CritterDB uses a step-by-step method for creating your custom creatures that gives you the power to customize every field while also supplying automated generators that will save you time and turn all of your creations into professional designs.

If you want to create your own creatures head over to the sign up page to complete the simple sign up form. Sign up is required so that your creatures can be saved in the cloud, but it is really quick and easy - I promise!

Once you have an account, you can create new creatures and they will be saved for you in the cloud for easy access from your mobile or desktop browser - even in the middle of a session!

You can even tag your favorite published bestiaries so that you can easily access them from your account in the future.
